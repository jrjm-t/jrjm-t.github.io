Orbits

Made using the Unity Game Engine, "Orbits" is a 2D game where you face off against endless waves of circular opponents known as "orbytes." However, your survival relies on your ability to manipulate the motion of orbs that (of course) orbit around you. Being able to control the distance of your orbs while dodging the orbs of your enemies is crucial to make it far in this endless wave survival.

Developing this game has taught me a lot about working with Unity2D physics, maximization of prefabs, design tools like Inkscapes, and even a little bit of how to make simple custom shaders.

Check out Orbits on itch.io
